Beauty and the Beast

The Beginning

Once upon a time, a merchant was traveling when he came across a mysterious castle. As he ventured inside, he was greeted by a Beast who demanded that he stay for dinner. The merchant, frightened, explained that he had a daughter, Belle, who would be devastated if he never returned.

The Beast agreed to let him go, but only if Belle would come in his place. The merchant reluctantly agreed to the Beast's terms.
