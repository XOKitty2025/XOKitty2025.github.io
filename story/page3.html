Beauty and the Beast

Belle's Arrival

Belle, courageous and kind, arrived at the castle. Initially frightened by the Beast's appearance, she soon learned that he was not as cruel as he seemed. The two began to communicate, and Belle discovered that the Beast was actually a prince under a curse. The curse could only be broken by true love, but time was running out. If no one loved the Beast before the last petal of the enchanted rose fell, the curse would be permanent.
